Temas de Inglés Interactivos

Used to / Solía

Se usa used to para describir hábitos o situaciones en el pasado que ya no ocurren.

  • I used to live in London.
  • She used to play the piano.
  • They used to go fishing every summer.

Frequency Adverbs / Adverbios de Frecuencia

Los adverbios de frecuencia expresan cuán seguido ocurre una acción.

  • I always wake up early.
  • She usually walks to work.
  • They sometimes eat out on weekends.
  • He never eats fast food.

Past Simple / Pasado Simple

El pasado simple se usa para describir acciones completadas en el pasado.

  • I visited Paris last year.
  • She went to the market yesterday.
  • They played football last weekend.

Past Continuous / Pasado Continuo

El pasado continuo describe acciones en progreso en un momento específico del pasado.

  • I was reading when the phone rang.
  • They were playing soccer at 3 PM.
  • She was cooking dinner.
