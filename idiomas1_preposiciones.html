Prepositions of Time, Place, and Movement / Preposiciones de tiempo, lugar y movimiento

Teoría

Las preposiciones conectan palabras, indicando relaciones de tiempo, lugar o movimiento.

Ejemplos

  1. She arrives at 8 PM.
  2. They were born in July.
  3. We will meet on Friday.
  4. The keys are on the desk.
  5. She jumped into the pool.

Ejercicios

Pregunta: I go to school ___ (in/on/at) the morning.

Pregunta: We arrived ___ (on/at/in) Monday.

State Verbs / Verbos de Estado

Teoría

Los verbos de estado describen sentimientos, pensamientos, posesión o percepción.

Ejemplos

  1. I love reading.
  2. She believes in magic.
  3. They have a new apartment.
  4. He hears music from the kitchen.

Ejercicios

Pregunta: He ___ (believe) in ghosts.

Pregunta: I ___ (not like) broccoli.

Present Simple / Presente Simple

Teoría

El presente simple describe hábitos y hechos generales.

Ejemplos

  1. I wake up at 7 AM.
  2. She works in an office.
  3. They play football on weekends.
  4. Water boils at 100°C.

Ejercicios

Pregunta: They ___ (play) tennis on Saturdays.

Pregunta: She ___ (not work) on Sundays.

Present Continuous / Presente Continuo

Teoría

El presente continuo se usa para acciones en progreso o planes próximos.

Ejemplos

  1. I am reading a novel right now.
  2. She is studying for her exam.
  3. They are going to the cinema tonight.
  4. He is playing football now.

Ejercicios

Pregunta: We ___ (have) dinner now.

Pregunta: He ___ (not watch) TV now.
